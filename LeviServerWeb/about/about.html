About Me

Electronics, Programming, Amateur Radio and Low Level Systems

Hi, I’m Ishir. I develop software and build electronics, ranging from analog RF and audio circuits to complex digital systems. I have a deep interest in low-level architecture, which often leads me to write code in assembly and design or follow digital circuits. Additionally, I enjoy amateur radio, which also influences what projects I work on.

This site serves as a technical journal of my engineering journey. It documents most of my projects, including my problems, the examples I create, and the schematics I design. I strive to make my work accessible by providing clear documentation, diagrams, and explanations for everything I build.
